A Web Design shop based in Wilmington NC.

What we do

We build websites. From simple landing pages or product sites to blogs and online stores, we build responsive designs using grid based approaches and frameworks.

Our designs look great on mobile while also scaling gracefully to desktop dimensions. Intuitive content management systems are utilized, allowing you to easily update and manage your site.

Already have a site? We can refresh or entirely reboot your online identity with a comprehensive overhaul. Have more complex needs? CMS migrations, bespoke dashboards to manage and visualize your business data, custom administrative portals for your employees and customers, or custom programming to connect a legacy system or data source to the web are all possibilities.

Straightforward contracts with fixed, understandable costs. Deliverables on-time and on-budget.

Engage us for a one off project or retain us for affordable ongoing support and design work for existing websites.

Our Process

Strategy

We start with a discussion. What are your goals? What message do you want to communicate?

Your website is the primary platform for engaging and retaining your audience. It needs to communicate a coherent brand and identity that reflects your organization’s values and leaves a strong positive impression on the first visit.

Design

We prototype your site’s look and feel: UX, typography, color palette. We define functional requirements, site organization and navigation.

Logo and overall branding can be created by us or we can integrate your established branding into a new design.

Development

Next we choose appropriate frameworks and a CMS and begin the process of translating the prototype into code to fully realize the design. Our code is prioritized for accessibility, performance, and responsiveness.

Deployment

We deliver final assets and code for integration into the server setup of your choice or we can deploy and manage your website for you, handling all the nitty-gritty IT details so you don’t have to.
